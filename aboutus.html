About Us (Bano Qabil Program)

THE DEMAND OF PAKISTAN'S DEMOGRAPHICS

With almost 65% of Pakistan's population younger than 30, there are more young people now in the country than ever, and the trend is expected to continue until 2050.

THE DREAM OF EDUCATION

Alkhidmat Karachi has stepped in with an ambitious and first-of-its-kind education initiative called "Bano Qabil" to help Karachi's youth. Bano Qabil offers 100% free, advanced IT courses packed with the latest technologies to give our youth a headstart in launching their employment and freelancing careers.

WHO IT WILL BENEFIT

More than 75,000 candidates registered themselves through the program's dedicated mobile app and website. Separate Aptitude Tests for boys and girls were held at the Bagh-e-Jinnah grounds.
